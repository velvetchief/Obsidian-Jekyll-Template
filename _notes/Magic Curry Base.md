Makes cooking curry a breeze. 


**Servings:** 4+  |  **Prep Time:** 2 min  |  **Cooking Time:** 10 min

## Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes
- 1 Onion
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1 TBSP Turmeric
- 1 cup Cashew

## Directions

1. Rough chop tomatoes and onion
2. Heat a pot on medium heat, add all the ingredients
3. Add enough water to barely cover the surface
4. Stir and bring to a gentle boil
5. Cover and cook until tomatoes are fully mushy, and onions are transparent and chunky, around 5 minutes
6. Drain excess water. Allow to come back to room temperature
7. Blend to a smooth paste

---

*Stays fresh in the refrigerator for upto 4 days, makes 3-4 curries to serve 12-16 people.*
