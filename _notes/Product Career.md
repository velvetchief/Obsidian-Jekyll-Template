*Ecommerce, Digital Ops, CX, Omnichannel, Merchandising, Retail, Marketplaces*

### Ed-tech Startup (2008-2010)

I was the first product guy at an ed-tech startup - a two sided marketplace for online tutoring.

- Modernized complete web platform to be web 2.0 compliant
- Responsible for $50K monthly SEM spend, SEO
- Implemented web and CX analytics
- Increased traffic from 9K/ month to 143K+/ month in 9 months
- Increased conversion from 3% to 17% through increased offerings, CX initiatives
- Started viral marketing with facebook pages - 200K+ fans, engaging content
- Implemented bundled pricing
- Implemented Live chat support
- Revenue increased from ~$300K to ~2.7M ARR
