**Servings:** 4  |  **Prep Time:** 2 min  |  **Cooking Time:** 20 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb Salmon, chopped
- 2 Tbsp Oil
- 3 Cloves
- 5 Cardamom
- 1 Cinnamon stick
- 1 tsp Cumin seeds
- 1 Red Chilli
- 1 tsp Ginger & Garlic paste
- 3 ladles curry base
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1 Tbsp Coriander powder
- 1 tsp Garam Masala
- 1 tsp Kashmiri Chilli powder
- 1/2 cup finely chopped cilantro
- 10 Curry Leaves (optional)

## Directions

1. Warm a pan with oil over medium heat
2. Drop in whole spices — let them bubble softly
3. Add curry leaves and a spoon of ginger-garlic paste; stir till the raw scent fades
4. Add the curry base, stir it in
5. Turn up the heat, sprinkle coriander powder and garam masala, stir for a breath
6. Pour in 1/2 cup water — or more for a looser flow — and simmer gently
7. Lower the flame, ease in chopped salmon and Kashmiri chili powder — stir with care, fish is tender
8. Cover and cook low, 5-10 minutes, until oil gleams atop
9. Finish with cilantro leaves
10. Serve with rice or bread, as you please

---

*Pairs well with crisp Pinot Grigio & Gin Martinis (stirred not shaken).*
