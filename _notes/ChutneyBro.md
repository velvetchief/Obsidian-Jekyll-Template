When I shut down [Velvet House](https://www.velvethouses.com/), the itch to do another food and beverage concept was at its peak. A coffee trip that spanned 6 Indonesian islands and the Malabar Highlands in India fueled this fire. 

Contrary to what people think, the worlds best coffee is grown in Indonesia, and India is not far behind. What about Central and Latin America? Well, they suck. Giga Cringe. 

For location, Berkeley won on paper - College town, East Bay jewel, diverse age demographics, plus old and new wealth - with taste. Two weeks on the ground and none of these stood - Berkeley was dull, boring, and full of people who looked like sheep and parrots - doing the same things, talking about the same stuff, no foot traffic/ parking - there's enough restaurants and coffee shops there already for these types. Not here.
