---
title: Chicken Pulav
tags: [familyrecipes, recipes]
---

1kg chicken aayinchufy

10 red chillies

Add rice 3 cups

Add curry leaves and salt

After 50 percent cooked add turmeric 1.5 tsp

After 60 percent add ginger 3/4 tbsp and garlic 1 tbsp

Garam Masala 2 - 3 tbsp

Add chicken

After 80 percent - Add garam 1TBSP

Anapa neelu- bayleaf, tamarind littul, star anise, marathi mocha cardamom and cinnamon, sauf

Boil in 7 glasses water
